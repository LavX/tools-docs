## üìñ How to Use

1. **Access the Tool**: Navigate to the [Open Graph Generator](https://tools.lavx.hu/tools/open-graph-generator) page.
2. **Fill in Core Open Graph Tags**: Enter your webpage information. These fields also populate the corresponding Twitter tags:
   - **Title**: The title of your page (`og:title` and `twitter:title`).
   - **Type**: Select the content type (e.g., website, article, product).
   - **Image URL**: Full URL to your preview image. Recommended size: **1200x630px**.
   - **Canonical URL**: The permanent URL of your page (`og:url` and `twitter:url`).
   - **Description**: A brief summary. Recommended length: **155-160 characters**.
   - **Site Name**: The name of your website (e.g., MyWebsite.com).
   - **Locale**: The language/region code (defaults to `en_US`).
3. **Configure Twitter Card Tags**: Adjust Twitter-specific settings:
   - **Twitter Card Type**: Choose the display style (defaults to `summary_large_image`).
   - **Twitter Site Handle**: The `@username` of the website.
   - **Twitter Creator Handle**: The `@username` of the content creator/author.
4. **Copy Generated Tags**: The HTML tags appear automatically in the output area.
5. **Use in Your HTML**: Copy the tags and paste them into the `<head>` section of your HTML page.

## üìã Input Fields Explained

### Core Open Graph Tags
- **Title**: The title of your shared content.
- **Type**: The type of content being shared (website, article, book, profile, video.movie, music.song, product).
- **Image URL**: URL to the image that represents your content.
- **URL**: The canonical URL of your page.
- **Description**: A brief description of your content.
- **Site Name**: The name of your website or application.
- **Locale**: The language and region code (e.g., `en_US`).

### Twitter Card Tags
- **Twitter Card Type**: Controls the style of Twitter's content preview (summary, summary_large_image, app, player).
- **Twitter Site Handle**: Your website's Twitter username.
- **Twitter Creator Handle**: The author's Twitter username.
