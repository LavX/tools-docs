## üõ†Ô∏è Key Features

### 1. ROI Calculator
Calculate the total and annualized return on any investment.
- **Inputs**: 
  - **Initial Investment ($)**: The amount of money originally invested.
  - **Final Value ($)**: The current or expected value of the investment.
  - **Investment Period (years)**: The duration of the investment in years.
- **Outputs**: 
  - **Total ROI**: The percentage return over the entire period.
  - **Annualized ROI (CAGR)**: The geometric mean return that would provide the same total return over the period.
  - **Total Profit/Loss**: The absolute dollar difference between final value and initial investment.

### 2. Compound Interest Calculator
Visualize the power of compounding with optional regular contributions.
- **Inputs**: 
  - **Initial Investment ($)**: Starting principal amount.
  - **Annual Interest Rate (%)**: Expected yearly return percentage.
  - **Time Period (years)**: Duration in years.
  - **Compounding Frequency**: How often interest is calculated and added (Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, Semi-Annually, Annually).
  - **Additional Contribution ($)**: Recurring amount added to the investment.
  - **Contribution Frequency**: How often contributions are made (Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, Semi-Annually, Annually).
- **Logic**: Contributions are added at the beginning of each period, and interest is applied at the end of each period.
- **Outputs**: 
  - **Final Balance**: The total value at the end of the period.
  - **Total Contributions**: Sum of the initial investment and all recurring additions.
  - **Total Interest Earned**: Total growth from interest alone.
  - **Year-by-Year Breakdown**: Table showing balance and interest for each year.

### 3. Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA) Simulator
Simulate regular investments into an asset over time to see the impact of consistent saving.
- **Inputs**: 
  - **Initial Investment ($)**: Starting amount (can be zero).
  - **Regular Investment Amount ($)**: Amount invested at each interval.
  - **Investment Frequency**: How often you invest (Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, Semi-Annually, Annually).
  - **Time Period (years)**: Total duration in years.
  - **Expected Annual Return (%)**: Projected yearly growth percentage.
- **Logic**: Regular investments are added at the beginning of each period, and growth is applied at the end of each period.
- **Outputs**: 
  - **Final Balance**: Total value of the portfolio.
  - **Total Invested**: Cumulative amount of all investments made.
  - **Total Growth**: The difference between final balance and total invested.
  - **Year-by-Year Breakdown**: Table showing balance, total invested, and growth for each year.

## üìñ How to Use

1. **Select Calculator Type**: Choose between **ROI Calculator**, **Compound Interest**, or **Dollar-Cost Averaging** using the tabs at the top.
2. **Enter Your Data**:
   - For **ROI**: Enter your initial investment, final value, and the time period.
   - For **Compound Interest**: Enter your starting principal, interest rate, time period, and any recurring contributions.
   - For **DCA**: Enter your starting amount, recurring investment amount, frequency, and expected return.
3. **Adjust Parameters**: Fine-tune the time period and frequencies to match your specific strategy.
4. **Analyze Results**: Review the summary cards for high-level totals and the year-by-year breakdown table for detailed growth tracking.
