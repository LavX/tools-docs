# Crypto Wallet Generator

The **Crypto Wallet Generator** is a specialized utility designed to demonstrate how deterministic wallet addresses can be derived from a seed value. It provides a visual and interactive way to understand address formats across different blockchain networks.

### Key Benefits
- **Educational Insight**: Learn about address prefixes and formats for various popular blockchains.
- **Deterministic Generation**: Understand how the same seed consistently produces the same set of addresses.
- **Multi-Network Support**: Explore addresses for 10 different blockchain ecosystems in one place.
- **Privacy-Focused**: All address generation happens locally in your browser; your seed never leaves your device.

## üìñ How to Use

1. **Access the Tool**: Navigate to the [Crypto Wallet Generator](https://tools.lavx.hu/tools/crypto-wallet) page.
2. **Set Your Seed**: Use the automatically generated random seed or enter your own custom string in the "Seed Value" input.

4. **Generate Addresses**: Click the "Generate Wallet Addresses" button to derive the addresses from your seed.
5. **Explore Results**: View the generated addresses, copy them to your clipboard, or click "Explorer" to see the address on a live blockchain explorer.
