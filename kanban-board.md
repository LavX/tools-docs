- **Intuitive Drag & Drop**: Effortlessly move tasks between columns or reorder them within a list.

- **Customizable Workflow**: Create, rename, and reorder columns to match your specific process.

## üìñ How to Use

1. **Access the Tool**: Navigate to the [Kanban Board](https://tools.lavx.hu/tools/kanban-board) page.
2. **Manage Columns**:
   - Click **"+ Add Column"** to create a new stage in your workflow.
   - Hover over a column header to see options for editing the title or deleting the column.
   - Drag a column by its header to reorder it.
3. **Manage Tasks**:
   - Click **"+ Add Task"** at the bottom of any column to create a new task.
   - Fill in the task details (Title is required) and click **"Add Task"**.
   - Click the **pencil icon (‚úèÔ∏è)** on any task to edit its details or delete it.
4. **Organize Your Workflow**:
   - Drag tasks by their handle to move them between columns or change their priority within a column.
   - Use the **Search bar** at the top to filter tasks as your board grows.
5. **Automatic Saving**: Your changes are saved instantly to your browser. You can close the tab and return later to find your board exactly as you left it.
