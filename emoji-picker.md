# Emoji Picker

A comprehensive and intuitive tool for finding, selecting, and managing emojis for your digital content.

**Emoji Picker** is a streamlined utility designed to help you quickly find the perfect emoji for any context. Whether you're drafting a social media post, writing an email, or developing an application, this tool provides a seamless experience for discovering and copying emojis.

### Key Benefits
- **Instant Copy**: Clicking any emoji immediately copies it to your clipboard for quick use.
- **Text Composition**: Build longer strings of emojis and text in the integrated editor.
- **Adaptive Interface**: Automatically switches between light and dark themes based on your system settings.
- **Privacy-First**: All processing and clipboard actions happen locally in your browser.

## üìñ How to Use

1.  **Access the Tool**: Navigate to the [Emoji Picker](https://tools.lavx.hu/tools/emoji-picker) page.
2.  **Find an Emoji**: Use the **Search** bar to look for specific emojis or browse through the **Categories**.
3.  **Select Skin Tone**: (Optional) Use the skin tone selector to choose your preferred representation.
4.  **Add to Text**: Click on an emoji to add it to the **Your Text** area. This action also automatically copies the individual emoji to your clipboard.
5.  **Manage Your Text**:
    -   **Edit**: You can manually type or edit text within the text area.
    -   **Clear**: Use the **Clear** button to empty the text area.
    -   **Copy All**: Click **Copy Text** to copy the entire contents of the text area to your clipboard.
