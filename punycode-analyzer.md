1. **Access the Tool**: Navigate to the [Punycode/IDN Analyzer](https://tools.lavx.hu/tools/punycode-analyzer) page.

1. **Switch to Batch Mode**: Click the "Batch Analysis" tab to analyze multiple domains at once.

3. **Process**: Click analyze to process all domains simultaneously.
4. **Review Statistics**: View comprehensive statistics and risk distribution across all domains.

1. **Browse Patterns**: Click the "Attack Library" tab to explore real-world attack examples.
2. **Filter by Category**: Filter patterns by category (banking, social media, tech companies, etc.).
3. **Select Pattern**: Click on any pattern to analyze it immediately.
4. **Search Patterns**: Use the search function to find specific attack patterns.

### Unicode/Punycode Converter
1. **Switch Converter**: Click the "Converter" tab for bidirectional conversion.
2. **Input Text**: Enter Unicode text or Punycode for conversion.
3. **View Results**: See both representations with detailed character analysis.
