## üìñ How to Use

1. **Access the Tool**: Navigate to the [Meeting Timezone Organizer](https://tools.lavx.hu/tools/meeting-scheduler) page.

2. **Add Participants** (Scheduler View):
   - Click "Add Participant" and search for a city or timezone
   - Give each participant a descriptive label (e.g., "New York Team", "Tokyo Office")
   - Configure default working hours (9 AM - 5 PM)
   - Add flexible hours if some participants can meet outside working hours

3. **Configure Meeting** (Scheduler View):
   - Add at least 2 participants (minimum required)
   - Select the meeting date (handles DST automatically for that date)
   - Choose meeting duration (30 min - 2 hours)
   - Set individual working hours and flexibility per participant

4. **View Timeline Comparison** (Compare View):
   - Switch to Compare View once you have 2+ participants and a date selected
   - See 24-hour timeline showing each participant's availability
   - Green indicates working hours, yellow shows flexible hours, gray shows unavailable
   - Click on any time slot to see it in local times for all participants

5. **Select Optimal Time** (Results View):
   - View ranked time slots sorted by availability score
   - Scores range from 0-100% based on how many participants are in working vs flexible hours
   - Click on any slot to see detailed breakdown
   - View meeting details in each participant's local timezone

6. **Share Meeting Details** (Results View):
   - Copy shareable text with meeting times in each participant's local timezone
   - Paste directly into emails, calendar invites, or Slack messages
   - Include date, duration, and time in each participant's local timezone
