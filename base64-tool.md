# Base64 Encoder/Decoder

A secure, browser-based utility for encoding text to Base64 and decoding Base64 back to plain text.

## üöÄ Why Use Base64 Tool?

**Base64 Tool** is a essential utility for developers and data analysts. It provides a reliable way to handle binary-to-text encoding without sending your sensitive data to a server.

### Key Benefits
- **Privacy-First**: All processing happens locally in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.
- **UTF-8 Support**: Correct handling of special characters and non-ASCII text.
- **File Integration**: Easily upload files for conversion or download your results.
- **Conversion History**: Keep track of your recent work with local storage persistence.

- **Dual Modes**: Seamlessly switch between **Encode to Base64** and **Decode from Base64**.
- **Flexible Input**: Type or paste text directly, or upload a file to read its content.
- **Instant Results**: Real-time feedback with error validation for invalid Base64 strings.
- **Export Options**: Copy results to your clipboard with one click or download them as a `.txt` file.
- **History Management**: Automatically saves your last 20 conversions. Review, reload, or clear your history at any time.

## üìñ How to Use

1. **Select Mode**: Choose either **Encode to Base64** or **Decode from Base64** at the top of the page.
2. **Provide Input**:
   - Type or paste your content into the text area.
   - Alternatively, click **Upload File** to load content from a local file.
3. **Convert**: Click the **Encode** or **Decode** button to process your data.
4. **Manage Output**:
   - View the result in the output section.
   - Click **Copy** to save the result to your clipboard.
   - Click **Download** to save the result as a text file.
5. **Review History**: Scroll down to see your previous conversions. Click any history item to reload it into the tool.
