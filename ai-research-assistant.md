# AI Research Assistant

The **AI Research Assistant** is a powerful, multi-agent research tool designed to provide deep insights, structured reports, and diverse perspectives on any given topic. It leverages advanced AI models to simulate a collaborative research environment, delivering comprehensive results that go far beyond simple search queries.

## üöÄ Why Use AI Research Assistant?

In an era of information overload, finding high-quality, balanced, and well-structured information is challenging. The AI Research Assistant streamlines this process by acting as a team of specialized agents that gather, analyze, and synthesize information for you.

### Key Benefits
- **Multi-Agent Collaboration**: Simulates experts with different roles to provide a well-rounded view.
- **Real-Time Progress**: Watch the research unfold with live activity logs and status updates.
- **Balanced Perspectives**: Automatically identifies main arguments and counterarguments.
- **Structured Output**: Receive a professional report organized into logical sections.
- **Export Ready**: Save your research in multiple formats including DOCX, PDF, HTML, and Markdown.

## üìñ How to Use

1. **Enter Your Topic**: Start by typing your research subject in the instruction stage.
2. **Configure Your Research**:
   - Select the **Research Depth** (Basic or Comprehensive).
   - Toggle **Include Sources** to see the underlying data.
   - Enable **Counterarguments** for a balanced view.
   - Add **Expert Perspectives** and **Expert Discussion** for deeper analysis.
   - Enable **CoStorm** for the most advanced research methodology.
3. **Monitor the Process**: Click "Start Research" and watch the live logs as AI agents perform searches and synthesize data.
4. **Review the Result**: Explore the structured report, switch between the main report and the sources tab, and navigate through sections like Overview, Key Facts, and Arguments.
5. **Export Your Work**: Use the export buttons to download your research as a DOCX, PDF, HTML, or Markdown file, or simply copy it to your clipboard.
