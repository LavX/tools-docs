1. **Select Language**: Choose the language spoken in your audio or video file from the dropdown menu.
2. **Upload File**: Drag and drop your file into the upload area or click to select a file from your device (Max 150MB).
3. **Start Transcription**: Click the **"Start Transcription"** button to begin the AI-powered process.
4. **Monitor Progress**: Watch the real-time progress bar as the AI analyzes your file.
5. **Review & Export**: Once complete, review the text in the output area. You can copy it to your clipboard or download it as a TXT or JSON file.
