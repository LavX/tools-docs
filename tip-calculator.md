- **Visual Feedback**: Clear presentation of tip amount, total amount, and per-person calculations
- **Time-Saving**: Get instant results as you adjust parameters

## üìñ How to Use

1. **Access the Tool**: Navigate to the [Tip Calculator](https://tools.lavx.hu/tools/tip-calculator) page.
2. **Enter Bill Amount**: Input the total bill amount and select your preferred currency.
3. **Set Tip Percentage**: Choose a tip percentage using preset buttons or enter a custom value.
4. **Split Bill (Optional)**: Specify the number of people to split the bill among.
5. **Configure Tax (Optional)**: Add tax as either a fixed amount or percentage, and choose whether tax is calculated before or after the tip.
6. **Apply Rounding (Optional)**: Select rounding direction for the per-person amount.
7. **View Results**: See calculated tip amount, total amount, and per-person share instantly.
8. **Copy Results**: Use the copy button to save or share calculation details.
9. **Access History**: View, reload, or delete previous calculations in the history section.
