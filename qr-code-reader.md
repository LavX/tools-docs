## üìñ How to Use

1. **Access the Tool**: Navigate to the [QR Code Reader](https://tools.lavx.hu/tools/qr-code-reader) page
2. **Choose Scanning Method**: Select either "Upload Image" or "Use Webcam"
3. **Scan Your QR Code**:
   - **Upload**: Drag & drop or click to select an image file (PNG, JPG, WEBP, GIF, max 5MB)
   - **Webcam**: Click "Start Webcam", grant camera permissions, and point at the QR code
4. **View Results**: The decoded content appears with intelligent parsing and action suggestions
5. **Take Action**: Use the context-specific action buttons (open links, copy data, send messages, etc.)
6. **Manage History**: View recent scans in the Scan History section and clear when needed
