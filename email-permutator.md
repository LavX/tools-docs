# Email Permutator

The **Email Permutator** is a specialized OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) tool designed to generate common corporate email address formats based on a person's name and a target domain. It is an essential utility for security researchers, penetration testers, and sales professionals looking to identify potential contact points.
