### 1. APY/APR Calculator
Convert Annual Percentage Rate (APR) to Annual Percentage Yield (APY) and project your total returns over time.

**Inputs:**
- **Principal Amount ($)**: Your initial investment.
- **Annual Rate (APR %)**: The nominal annual interest rate.
- **Compounding Frequency**: Choose how often interest is added (Annually, Quarterly, Monthly, Weekly, Daily, or Hourly).
- **Time Period (Years)**: The duration of your investment.

**Calculated Results:**
- **Effective Annual Yield (APY)**: The actual annual return taking compounding into account.
- **Final Balance**: The total value of your investment at the end of the period.
- **Interest Earned**: The total profit generated.
- **Compound Interest Effect**: The percentage increase in returns compared to simple interest.
