# Data Entry Practice

Improve your data entry speed and accuracy with professional-grade practice exercises designed for various data types.

## üöÄ Why Use Data Entry Practice?

**Data Entry Practice** is a specialized utility for anyone looking to enhance their typing precision and speed. Whether you are preparing for a data entry role or just want to improve your productivity, this tool provides realistic scenarios and detailed performance metrics.

### Key Benefits
- **Realistic Data Types**: Practice with numbers, addresses, phone numbers, and more.
- **Instant Feedback**: See your WPM, CPM, and accuracy in real-time.
- **Progress Tracking**: Your session history is saved locally so you can monitor your improvement.
- **Privacy-First**: All data generation and processing happen entirely in your browser.

## üõ†Ô∏è Key Features

### Multiple Practice Modes
- **Numbers**: Focus on numeric keypad or top-row number entry.
- **Words**: Standard English word practice for general typing speed.
- **Mixed Alphanumeric**: Practice with complex strings containing both letters and numbers.
- **Phone Numbers**: Realistic international and local phone number formats.
- **Addresses**: Full street addresses to simulate real-world data entry tasks.
- **Codes**: Practice with UUIDs and other technical identifiers.
- **Custom Text**: Paste your own text to practice specific documents or terminology.

### Customizable Difficulty
- **Easy**: Shorter segments and simpler data.
- **Medium**: Standard length and complexity.
- **Hard**: Long, complex strings and advanced data formats.

### Performance Metrics
- **WPM (Words Per Minute)**: Standardized typing speed calculation.
- **CPM (Characters Per Minute)**: Precise character-level speed tracking.
- **Accuracy Percentage**: Real-time calculation of your precision.
- **Error Tracking**: Visual identification of mistakes as you type.

### Session Management
- **Time Limits**: Set a specific duration (e.g., 60 seconds) or practice without a timer.
- **Pause/Resume**: Flexibility to pause your session and resume when ready.
- **Local History**: View a detailed log of your past sessions, including date, mode, and performance.

## üìñ How to Use

1. **Select Mode**: Choose a practice mode (e.g., Numbers, Addresses) from the settings.
2. **Set Difficulty**: Adjust the difficulty level to match your skill.
3. **Configure Timer**: (Optional) Set a time limit in seconds. Set to 0 for unlimited practice.
4. **Start Practice**: Click "Start Practice" or simply begin typing in the input area.
5. **Type Accurately**: Follow the displayed text. Correct characters turn green, while errors are highlighted in red.
6. **Review Results**: Once finished, analyze your WPM, CPM, and accuracy. Your results are automatically saved to your local history.
