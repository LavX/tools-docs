1.  **Describe Your Intent**: Enter the main purpose of your email in the "Email Intent" field (up to 1,000 characters).
2.  **Add Key Points (Optional)**: List any specific details or requirements you want included in the message.
3.  **Configure Settings**:

5.  **Review & Refine**: Read the generated email. You can regenerate it if you want a different variation.
6.  **Export or Copy**: Use the export buttons to download the file or click "Copy" to use it in your email client.
