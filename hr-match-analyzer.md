# AI HR Match Analyzer

AI-powered tool to analyze and match resumes with job descriptions for HR professionals and recruiters.

### AI-Powered Precision
- **Contextual Understanding**: Goes beyond keyword matching to evaluate the actual relevance of skills and experience.
- **Multi-Stage Analysis**: Performs deep dives into both job requirements and candidate profiles for maximum accuracy.
- **Actionable Intelligence**: Provides specific strengths, weaknesses, and interview recommendations.

## üìñ How to Use

1. **Access the Tool**: Open the [AI HR Match Analyzer](https://tools.lavx.hu/tools/hr-match-analyzer).
2. **Input Job Description**: Upload one or more job description files or paste the requirements text.
3. **Input Candidate Resume**: Upload the candidate's CV/resume or paste their profile text.
4. **Configure Settings**: 
    - Choose between **Basic** or **Comprehensive** analysis depth.
    - Adjust the **Weights** for Skills, Experience, and Education to match your priorities.
5. **Run Analysis**: Click the **"Run AI Match Analysis"** button.
6. **Review Results**:
    - Check the **Overall Match Score** and compatibility label.
    - Explore the **Match Breakdown** for specific categories.
    - Read the **AI Insights** for a qualitative summary.
    - Review **Strengths**, **Weaknesses**, and **Recommendations**.
7. **Reset**: Use the "Perform New Analysis" button to start over.
