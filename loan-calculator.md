# Loan Calculator

A comprehensive suite of financial tools to calculate loan payments, generate detailed amortization schedules, and plan early payoff strategies for various loan types.

## üöÄ Why Use Loan Calculator?

Managing debt requires precision and foresight. The **Loan Calculator** empowers you to understand the long-term impact of interest rates, payment frequencies, and additional payments, helping you make informed financial decisions without complex spreadsheets.

### Precision & Clarity
- **Multi-Mode Analysis**: Switch between standard payment calculation, amortization schedules, and early payoff planning.
- **Flexible Frequencies**: Support for monthly, bi-weekly, and weekly payment schedules.
- **Visual Progress**: Real-time summaries and progress bars to visualize principal vs. interest and time saved.
- **Instant Results**: All calculations are performed locally in your browser for immediate feedback.

## üõ†Ô∏è Key Features

### 1. Loan Payment Calculator
Calculate your recurring payments for different loan types including:
- **Mortgage Loans**
- **Auto Loans**
- **Personal Loans**
- **Student Loans**

**Inputs:** Loan amount, annual interest rate, loan term (years), and payment frequency.
**Outputs:** Periodic payment amount, total interest paid, and total cost of the loan.

### 2. Amortization Schedule
Generate a complete breakdown of your loan's lifecycle.
- **Detailed Table**: View payment number, principal paid, interest paid, and remaining balance for every period.
- **Dynamic Updates**: Adjust loan parameters and see the schedule update instantly.

### 3. Early Payoff Calculator
Discover how much you can save by paying more than the minimum.
- **Savings Analysis**: Calculate exactly how much interest you'll save by adding extra monthly payments.
- **Time Reduction**: See how many years and months you can shave off your loan term.
- **Payoff Progress**: Compare your original payoff timeline with your new, accelerated schedule.

## üìñ How to Use

1. **Select Calculator Type**: Choose between **Loan Payment Calculator**, **Amortization Schedule**, or **Early Payoff Calculator**.
2. **Enter Loan Details**:
   - For standard calculations, enter the loan amount, interest rate, and term.
   - For early payoff, enter your current balance and the additional monthly amount you plan to pay.
3. **Adjust Frequency**: (Standard mode only) Choose between Monthly, Bi-Weekly, or Weekly payments.
4. **Review Summary**: Analyze the payment summary or payoff progress displayed on the right (or below on mobile).
5. **Analyze Schedule**: Switch to the Amortization tab to see the period-by-period breakdown.
