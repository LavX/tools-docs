# Gas Fee Estimator

Estimate transaction fees across multiple blockchain networks using simulated network data and customizable parameters.

## üöÄ Why Use Gas Fee Estimator?

**Gas Fee Estimator** provides a comprehensive view of potential network costs, helping you understand and plan your blockchain transactions. Whether you're sending ETH, swapping tokens, or minting NFTs, this tool helps you visualize how different priority levels affect transaction costs across various ecosystems.

### Key Benefits
- **Multi-Network Simulation**: Compare estimated fees across **Ethereum**, **Polygon**, **Arbitrum**, **Optimism**, and **Base**.
- **Transaction Presets**: Quickly calculate costs for common actions like ETH transfers, ERC-20 swaps, and NFT mints using standard gas limits.
- **Fiat Conversion**: View estimates in both native tokens and fiat currencies (**USD/EUR**) based on simulated market rates.
- **Priority Levels**: Get clear cost breakdowns for **Low**, **Medium**, and **High** priority transactions.

## üìñ How to Use

1. **Select Network**: Choose the blockchain network you want to simulate fees for (e.g., Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, or Base).
2. **Choose Transaction Type**: Select a preset (like "ETH Transfer" or "Swap on DEX") or choose "Custom" to enter a specific gas limit.
3. **Set Custom Limit**: If "Custom" is selected, enter the desired gas limit (minimum 21,000).
4. **Toggle Currency**: Switch between **USD** and **EUR** to see fiat estimates based on simulated market prices.
5. **Analyze Results**: Review the Low, Medium, and High priority estimates to understand the cost implications of different network conditions.
