## üìñ How to Use

### Creating Regular Pastes (Anonymous or Authenticated)
1. **Access the Tool**: Navigate to the [Pastebin](https://tools.lavx.hu/tools/pastebin) page.
2. **Input Your Content**: Enter your code or text in the content area (up to 1MB).
3. **Configure Options**:
   - Set title, language (auto-detect or manual selection), file name, and description.
   - Choose visibility: Public (visible to all), Unlisted (link only), Private (password protected).
   - Set expiration: 1 hour, 1 day, 1 week, 1 month, or never
4. **Create Paste**: Click "Create Paste" to generate your shareable link.
5. **Manage Access**: For private pastes, set a password (minimum 8 characters).

### Creating E2EE Encrypted Pastes (Authenticated Users Only)
1. **Authenticate**: Log in to enable E2EE mode.
2. **Switch Mode**: Toggle to "E2EE" mode using the mode selector.
3. **Enter Content**: Fill in your content with optional title, language, description, and filename.
4. **Encrypt & Store**: Click "Create E2EE Paste" - your data is encrypted client-side before cloud sync.
5. **Share Securely**: Use the generated secure URL to share your encrypted paste.

### Viewing and Managing Pastes
- **Browse Pastes**: Access recent, public, or your personal pastes via the sidebar previews.
- **View All**: Use the "View All" links to browse complete paste collections.
- **Delete Protection**: Anonymous regular pastes include deletion passwords for later removal. Logged-in users can manage and delete their pastes directly from their account dashboard.
