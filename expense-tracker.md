## üìñ How to Use

1.  **Access the Tool**: Navigate to the [Expense Tracker](https://tools.lavx.hu/tools/expense-tracker) page.
2.  **Set Your Currency**: Use the Currency Settings panel to select your preferred currency.
3.  **Add Transactions**:
    - Enter the **Date** of the transaction.
    - Input the **Amount**.
    - Provide a brief **Description**.
    - Select the **Type** (Income or Expense).
    - Choose the appropriate **Category**.
    - Click **Add Transaction**.
4.  **Manage Your Data**:
    - Use the **Edit** icon to modify existing transactions.
    - Use the **Delete** icon to remove entries.
5.  **Filter & Analyze**: Use the filter controls above the transaction list to view specific time periods or categories.
6.  **Export Reports**: Click the **Export to PDF** or **Export to Excel** buttons to save your filtered data.
