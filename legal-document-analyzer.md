## üìñ How to Use

1. **Access the Tool**: Navigate to the [AI Legal Document Analyzer](https://tools.lavx.hu/tools/legal-document-analyzer) page.
2. **Provide Your Document**: 
   - Paste the text into the input area (up to 15,000 characters).

3. **Select Document Type**: Choose the category that best fits your document for more accurate results. If your document type isn't listed, select **Other** and specify it manually.
4. **Choose Analysis Type**: Select between Full Analysis, Summary, Risk Assessment, or Plain Language.
5. **Run Analysis**: Click **Analyze Document** and wait a few seconds for the AI to process.
6. **Review & Copy**: Read the analysis and use the **Copy** button to save the results.
