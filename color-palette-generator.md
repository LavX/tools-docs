### 1. Precise Color Input
- **Multiple Formats**: Input your base color using HEX, RGB (0-255), or HSL (0-360, 0-100%) values.
- **Visual Picker**: Use the native system color picker for intuitive selection.

### 2. Advanced Palette Generation
- **Harmony Types**: Choose from six different harmony rules:
    - **Complementary**: Two colors opposite each other on the color wheel.
    - **Analogous**: Three colors next to each other on the color wheel.
    - **Triadic**: Three colors evenly spaced around the color wheel.
    - **Tetradic (Square)**: Four colors evenly spaced around the color wheel.
    - **Monochromatic**: Different shades and tints of the same base color.
    - **Split Complementary**: A base color and the two colors adjacent to its complement.
- **Fine-Tuning**: Adjust **Brightness** and **Saturation** (-50% to +50%) to refine the generated colors.
- **Inspire Me**: Generate a completely random palette with a single click.

### 3. Accessibility Analysis
- **Contrast Ratios**: Real-time calculation of contrast ratios against white and black backgrounds.
- **WCAG Compliance**: Instant validation for AA and AAA standards for both normal and large text.

### 4. Export & Integration
- **CSS Variables**: Export your palette as ready-to-use CSS custom properties.
- **JSON**: Get a structured data representation of your palette including HEX, RGB, and HSL values.
- **Image (PNG)**: Download a high-resolution image of your palette for presentations or style guides.

### 5. Favorites Management
- **Save Palettes**: Store your favorite combinations with custom names.
- **Persistent Storage**: Your saved palettes are kept in your browser's local storage, so they're available when you return.
