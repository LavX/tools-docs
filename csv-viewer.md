# CSV Viewer & Editor

A powerful, browser-based utility to view, edit, and analyze CSV data with advanced features like filtering, sorting, and chart visualization.

## üöÄ Why Use CSV Viewer & Editor?

**CSV Viewer & Editor** is a comprehensive tool designed for developers, data analysts, and anyone working with structured text data. It provides a spreadsheet-like experience directly in your browser without the need for heavy software.

### Key Benefits
- **Interactive Editing**: Modify cells, add rows, or delete columns with ease.
- **Advanced Data Analysis**: Filter and sort your data to find exactly what you need.
- **Instant Visualization**: Turn your data into Bar, Line, or Pie charts instantly.
- **Privacy-First**: All processing happens locally in your browser; your data never leaves your device.
- **Seamless Integration**: Share your CSVs via Pastebin or save them to your local history.

## üõ†Ô∏è Key Features

- **Robust Parsing**: Powered by PapaParse for reliable handling of various CSV formats and edge cases.
- **Flexible Delimiters**: Supports Comma (`,`), Semicolon (`;`), Tab (`\t`), and Pipe (`|`).
- **Dynamic Table Interface**:
  - **Cell Editing**: Click any cell to update its value.
  - **Structure Management**: Add or remove rows and columns dynamically.
  - **Smart Sorting**: Toggle between ascending, descending, and original order. Supports both numeric and alphabetical sorting.
- **Powerful Filtering**: Apply multiple conditions such as "contains", "equals", "starts with", "greater than", and "less than".
- **Data Visualization**:
  - Generate **Bar**, **Line**, and **Pie** charts.
  - Select specific columns for X and Y axes.
- **File Management**:
  - **Import**: Load `.csv` or `.txt` files.
  - **Export**: Download your modified data as a `.csv` file.
  - **History**: Save multiple versions of your work to local storage for later access.
  - **Share**: Directly export your data to the [Pastebin](https://tools.lavx.hu/tools/pastebin) tool for easy sharing.

## üìñ How to Use

1. **Input Data**: Paste your CSV text into the input area or click **Import File** to upload a document.
2. **Configure Parsing**: Select the correct **Delimiter** and toggle the **Has Header** option if your data includes a header row.
3. **Edit Data**:
   - Click any cell to edit its content.
   - Use the **Edit Structure** section to add new rows or columns.
   - Hover over headers or rows to find the delete (‚úï) buttons.
4. **Analyze & Filter**:
   - Click column headers to sort data.
   - Use the **Filters** section to add one or more conditions to narrow down your view.
5. **Visualize**: Click **Show Chart**, select your chart type and axes to see a visual representation of your data.
6. **Save & Share**:
   - Click **Save** to keep a copy in your browser's history.
   - Click **Export CSV** to download the file.
   - Click **Share** to send the data to the Pastebin tool.
