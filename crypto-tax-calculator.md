# Crypto Tax Calculator

Calculate capital gains tax, import transaction history, and generate tax reports for cryptocurrency investments using professional accounting methods.

**Crypto Tax Calculator** is a specialized utility designed to simplify the complex process of calculating tax obligations for cryptocurrency trades. It provides a clear overview of your capital gains and estimated tax liabilities, helping you stay compliant with tax regulations.

### Key Benefits
- **Multiple Accounting Methods**: Choose between FIFO, LIFO, or HIFO to optimize your tax position.
- **Bulk Import**: Quickly upload your transaction history via CSV files.
- **Real-Time Calculation**: See your capital gains and estimated tax update instantly as you adjust settings.
- **Privacy-First**: All calculations are performed locally in your browser; your sensitive financial data never leaves your device.

## üõ†Ô∏è Key Features

- **Advanced Tax Methods**:
  - **FIFO (First In, First Out)**: Uses the oldest purchases first.
  - **LIFO (Last In, First Out)**: Uses the most recent purchases first.
  - **HIFO (Highest In, First Out)**: Uses the highest-priced purchases first to minimize taxable gains.
- **Transaction Management**:
  - Support for various transaction types: Buy, Sell, Transfer, Income, and Expense.
  - Detailed transaction history view with asset, quantity, price, and total value.
- **Comprehensive Reporting**:
  - Annual tax summary for the selected tax year.
  - Estimated tax liability based on customizable tax rates.
  - Placeholders for exporting CSV reports and IRS Form 8949 data.
- **CSV Import Support**:
  - Easily import data with required headers: `Date`, `Type`, `Asset`, `Quantity`, and `Price`.
  - Optional `Fee` column support for more accurate cost basis calculation.

## üìñ How to Use

1. **Access the Tool**: Navigate to the [Crypto Tax Calculator](https://tools.lavx.hu/tools/crypto-tax-calculator) page.
2. **Import Transactions**: 
   - Click **Upload CSV** to import your transaction history.
   - Ensure your CSV includes the required headers: `Date`, `Type`, `Asset`, `Quantity`, and `Price`.
3. **Configure Tax Settings**:
   - Select the **Tax Year** you wish to calculate for.
   - Choose your preferred **Calculation Method** (FIFO, LIFO, or HIFO).
   - Enter your applicable **Tax Rate (%)**.
4. **Review Summary**: View the **Tax Summary** section for your total transactions, capital gains, and estimated tax due.
5. **Generate Reports**: Use the **Tax Reports** tab to view annual summaries or export your transaction history.
