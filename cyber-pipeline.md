## ğŸ“– How to Use

1. **Enter Input**: Paste or type your raw data into the **Input** area at the top.
2. **Build Your Pipeline**:
   - Click the **Add Operation** button.
   - Select an operation from the dropdown menu (e.g., "Base64 Encode").
   - Add as many operations as needed. They will execute in the order they appear.
3. **Manage Operations**:
   - Remove an operation by clicking the **X** on its block.
   - Clear the entire chain using the **Clear All** button.
4. **View & Copy Results**:
   - The final transformed data appears in the **Output** section.
   - Click the **Copy** button to save the result to your clipboard.
