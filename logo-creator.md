### Input Parameters
- **Brand Name**: The primary text for the logo.
- **Tagline**: (Optional) A short phrase to be incorporated into the design.
- **Brand Story**: Essential context for the AI to understand the personality and audience.
- **Industry**: (Optional) Helps the AI choose appropriate industry-standard symbols and colors.
- **Keywords**: (Optional) Specific themes or concepts to emphasize.
- **Color Palette**: (Optional) Preferred colors or hex codes.
- **Additional Notes**: (Optional) Specific symbol ideas, typography preferences, or references.
- **Logo Styles** (Select up to 4):
    - *Minimalist*: Focus on simplicity and clarity.
    - *Geometric*: Symmetry and abstract symbolism.
    - *Lettermark*: Stylized initials or monograms.
    - *Emblem*: Badge or crest compositions.
    - *Mascot*: Character-driven designs.
    - *Futuristic*: High-tech aesthetic with gradients.
    - *Handcrafted*: Organic, artisan feel.
    - *Luxury*: Elegant typography and premium detailing.
- **Canvas Size**: Choose between 512x512, 768x768, or 1024x1024.

### Output Formats
- **PNG Original**: High-quality raster image with the generated background.
- **PNG Transparent**: Background-removed version for immediate use.
- **SVG Original**: Scalable vector version of the original image.
- **SVG Transparent**: Scalable vector version of the transparent image.
